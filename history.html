The origin of the name "Cloud House" seems to go back to a tenant who would winterize the property by putting boards up over the windows, and painting clouds on them. There was also a door on the second floor which was painted with clouds on it.

Hauntings

Swan's Island has many stories of hauntings and the Cloud House figures prominently in them. In a local podcast there was a spirited (pun intended) discussion of recent paranormal activity occuring during the demolition work this winter, which also included a reference to the Cloud House as "one of the most notoriously haunted houses on the island".
