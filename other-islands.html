Surrounding Islands

Black Island, Placentia Island

These two islands lay across the Eastern Passage, just over a mile off our shorefront.

Marshall Island

This island is to the west of Swan's and is notable for an abandoned airfield.

Hat Island

A small private island with rumored hiking trails, open to the considerate public

Isle au Haut

A bit to the west of Swan's Island but a sizable island that is partially Acadia park land with good hiking avalable

Long Island(Frenchboro)

This island is to the south of Swan's Island and has lunch available at Lunds

the Cranberries

These islands lay just to the south of Southwest Harbor. They both have some level of restaurants and enough attractions to warrant a day of exploration
